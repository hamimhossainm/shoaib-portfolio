    What is Data preprocessing ?

    Data preprocessing involves several essential steps to prepare raw data for analysis. These steps include cleaning the data by removing errors and inconsistencies, reducing noise to improve data quality, and fixing missing values to ensure completeness. Additionally, scaling and encoding the data standardizes formats and removes duplicates, while handling outliers mitigates the impact of excessive values.

    What I provide ?

    • Encoding the data

    • Fixing the excessive values (outlier)
